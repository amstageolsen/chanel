Fine Jewellery

– A WOMAN. A DESTINY. A JEWELLERY.

CHANEL Fine Jewellery creations are inspired by the bold character of Mademoiselle Chanel’s first jewellery collection in 1932, as well as the strong themes that symbolise the brand’s identity. Between 1988 and 2007, Lorenz Bäumer designed collections of High Jewellery and Fine Jewellery for CHANEL. Click here to discover these pieces of jewellery. Since then, the Maison has developed its own Studio of Creation. From the first sketches which draw on the world of Gabrielle Chanel to a very detailed artistic drawing of a piece of jewellery destined for the workshops, the Studio of Creation forever reinvents the world of CHANEL Fine Jewellery.
